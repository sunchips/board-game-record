# King of New York

- **Slug:** `king-of-new-york`
- **Year published:** 2014
- **Designer:** Richard Garfield

Monsters tear through the five boroughs, rolling dice to smash units and buildings, hoard energy, and chase the Superstar badge. First to 20 Stars — or the last monster left standing — wins.
