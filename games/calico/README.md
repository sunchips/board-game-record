# Calico

- **Slug:** `calico`
- **Year published:** 2020
- **Designer:** Kevin Russ

Players sew quilts by placing hexagonal patch tiles on their personal board, trying to attract cats (which prefer specific color/pattern sets), complete sets of same-color patches (earning buttons), and satisfy three design-goal tiles tucked into the quilt. Highest total VP at game end wins.
