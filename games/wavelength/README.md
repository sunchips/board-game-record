# Wavelength

- **Slug:** `wavelength`
- **Year published:** 2019
- **Designers:** Wolfgang Warsch, Alex Hague, Justin Vickers

A social-guessing party game. Each round a psychic sees a hidden target on a spectrum dial (e.g. "Cold ↔ Hot") and gives a one-sentence clue; teammates try to land their guess on the target, scoring 4 / 3 / 2 for bullseye / inner / outer zones. The opposing team also guesses left/right for 1 bonus point. First team to **10 points** wins.
