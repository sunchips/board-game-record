# Jaipur

- **Slug:** `jaipur`
- **Year published:** 2009
- **Designer:** Sébastien Pauchon

Two traders in the market of Jaipur race to become the Maharaja's personal trader. Players take and sell sets of six goods over a best-of-three match; the first to win two rounds (Seals of Excellence) wins the match.

- [`jaipur.schema.json`](jaipur.schema.json) / [`jaipur.md`](jaipur.md) — base game.
