# Bunny Kingdom

- **Slug:** `bunny-kingdom`
- **Year published:** 2017
- **Designer:** Richard Garfield

Players lead rabbit clans across a 10×10 map, drafting cards each round to place fiefs, cities, towers, and resources. After four rounds, each player scores their connected fiefs (cities × resource types) plus a hand of secret Golden Parchments.
